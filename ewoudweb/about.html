About Me

I am a 13 year old middle schooler in Massachusetts. I mainly like electronics, engineering, coding, 3d printing, and cats. I have been doing electronics and engineering since I was 5, when I got an Arduino kit. I also participate in Hack Club, where I got a $100 grant to work on one of my projects, a massive tank, and a $100 grant to order custom PCBs that I design. I sometimes post videos on my youtube channel, Ewoud Van Vooren. My most watched long-form video has 1.7k views, and I have about 130 subscribers. I have 2 cats, Luna, who is 2 years old, and Cosmo, who is 1 year old. I was in a group that launched a weather baloon with one of my projects on it, a project to analyse current tree coverage and compares it to past coverage. I am in the leadership team for my local makerspace, and I do multiple classes there, including Latin.
